About Me

I am an undergraduate computer science major that is currently aiming to become a software engineer. The main reason for my investment in software engineering is because I have always enjoyed problem solving, particularly when it comes to creating products for others to use. The feeling of watching a piece of software you have worked on slowly come into fruition is captivating. This website is a way for me to learn web development while also sharing my projects on Github. In addition, some of my notes from my various classes will be stored on here for easy access.
